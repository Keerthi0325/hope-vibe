MAAVEERAN

Maaveeran is a 2023 Indian Tamil-language superhero film directed by Madonne Ashwin and produced by Arun Viswa. The film stars Sivakarthikeyan and Aditi Shankar, while Saritha, Mysskin, Monisha Blessy, Yogi Babu and Sunil play supporting roles.The film was officially announced in July 2022, along with the official title. Principal photography commenced in August 2022 in Chennai, followed with a schedule in Pondicherry, and wrapped by early-June 2023. The music was composed by Bharath Sankar, while the cinematography and editing were handled by Vidhu Ayyanna and Philomin Raj.
