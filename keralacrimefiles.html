KERALA CRIME FILES

Kerala Crime Files - Shiju, Parayil Veedu, Neendakara is an Indian Malayalam-language crime drama web television series directed by Ahammed Khabeer and written by Ashiq Aimar starring Aju Varghese and Lal in lead roles.Season 1 explores the Ernakulam North police station officers race against the time to catch a murderer out on loose.
