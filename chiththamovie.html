CHITHTHA

Chithha is a 2023 Indian Tamil-language drama film directed by S. U. Arun Kumar and produced by Siddharth, who stars in the lead role alongside with Nimisha Sajayan (in her Tamil debut) play female lead, in addition to Baby Sahasra Shree and Baby S. Aafiyah Tasneem in the prominent roles.

RATINGS:4.3/5
