VADHANDHI : THE FABLE OF VELONIE

Vadhandhi: The Fable of Velonie is a 2022 Indian-Tamil-language mystery crime thriller streaming television series created by Andrew Louis, of Leelai and Kolaigaran fame, for Amazon Prime Video. It was directed and written by Andrew Louis. The principal cast of the series includes S. J. Suryah, Laila, Nassar, Sanjana Krishnamoorthy, Smruthi Venkat, Vivek Prasanna, Kumaran Thangarajan, Vaibhav Murugesan, Vikky Aadithya and Hareesh Peradi. It premiered on 2 December 2022 and consists of eight episodes.

RATINGS:3.5/5
