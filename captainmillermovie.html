CAPTAIN MILLER

Captain Miller is an upcoming Indian Tamil-language action adventure film directed by Arun Matheswaran, and produced by Sathya Jyothi Films. It stars Dhanush in the title role, with Shiva Rajkumar, Priyanka Arul Mohan, Aditi Balan, Sundeep Kishan, Edward Sonnenblick and John Kokken in supporting roles.

RATINGS:4.2/5
