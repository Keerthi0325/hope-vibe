24

24 is a 2016 Indian Tamil-language science fiction action film written and directed by Vikram Kumar. Based on the concept of time travel, the film stars Suriya in a triple role alongside Samantha Ruth Prabhu and Nithya Menen. In the film, Sethuraman, a scientist invents a time traveler watch which leads to a bitter battle between his evil twin brother Athreya and his lookalike son Mani in the future.
