KALKI

Kalki is a 2019 Indian Malayalam-language action crime film directed by Praveen Prabharam and produced by Suvin K. Varkey and Prasobh Krishna for their studio Little Big Films.The film stars Tovino Thomas in the lead role, while Shivajith Padmanabhan, Samyuktha Menon and Vini Vishwa Lal appear in supporting roles. In the film, a cop named K takes charge at a village named Nanchenkotta, where he tries to restore peace and locks horns with a political leader.

RATINGS:3/5
