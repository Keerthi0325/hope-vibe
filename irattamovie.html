IRATTA

Iratta is a 2023 Malayalam-language crime thriller-drama film written and directed by Rohit M. G. Krishnan featuring Joju George in dual roles, Anjali, Arya Salim and Srikant Murali.The film revolves around the death of a policeman during his duty hours in Vagamon police station and the investigation that follows by his estranged twin brother.

RATINGS:4/5
