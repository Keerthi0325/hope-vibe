HOPE & VIBE



G. V. Prakash Kumar is an Indian music composer, playback singer, actor and producer known for his work in Tamil films and a few Telugu films as well. His first film was S Pictures' Veyil and he gained fame in Tamil films by the early 2010s. He made his debut in acting on 2015 through Darling.He has won one National Award and three Filmfare Awards in his film career.
