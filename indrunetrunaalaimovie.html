INDRU NETRU NAALAI

Indru Netru Naalai is a 2015 Indian Tamil-language science fiction comedy film written and directed by R. Ravikumar in his debut feature. The film is jointly produced by Thirukumaran Entertainment and Studio Green, and stars Vishnu Vishal and Miya, while Karunakaran, T. M. Karthik, P. Ravi Shankar and Jayaprakash play supporting roles. The film was released on 26 June 2015 and received positive reviews from critics.
